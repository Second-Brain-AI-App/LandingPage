Second Brain — Privacy Policy (US‑only)

Version: 0.1

Effective: 09/30/2025

Plain‑English summary (not legally binding): Second Brain stores your notes and embeddings on your device by default. When you ask a question, the app sends only your question and a few relevant snippets to our AI provider to generate an answer. You can export or delete your data at any time. Optional features like cloud backup, diagnostics, or marketing are opt‑in.

1) Who we are

Operator / Controller: Second Brain ("we," "us," or "our").
Email: 2ndbrain.app@gmail.com

This policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the Second Brain mobile application, website(s), and related services (the "Service"). The Service is offered only in the United States and is intended for U.S. users.

2) What we collect

We collect only what's necessary to operate the Service and what you optionally choose to share.

A. Data you add (User Content)

  • Notes, images, audio recordings (and transcripts), tags, and other content you capture.
  • On‑device embeddings generated from your content to enable fast local search.

B. App/account data

  • Account identifiers (e.g., Apple ID, email if provided).
  • Basic device info (device model, OS version) for support.

C. Usage & diagnostics (optional)

  • Crash logs and performance metrics only if you enable diagnostics.

D. Subscription & payments

  • Plan, status, and purchase receipts (processed by Apple; we do not receive your full payment details).

E. Support communications

  • Emails you send us and related metadata.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive categories (e.g., precise location, contacts, photos) unless you explicitly provide them inside your notes or grant OS‑level permissions for capture features.

3) Where your data lives (on‑device by default)

  • On‑device storage: Your raw notes and embeddings are stored locally on your device.
  • Q&A requests: When you ask a question, the app sends only your question and the top‑K matching snippets (plus minimal metadata like timestamps or tags) to our AI provider to generate an answer.
  • Cloud backup/sync (optional): If you enable it, a secure, encrypted copy of your data is stored with [cloud provider] in [region]. You can disable sync at any time.

4) How we use data (purposes)

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service (including embeddings and search).
  • Generate answers to your questions using the process described above.
  • Process subscriptions and manage access.
  • Communicate with you about the Service (e.g., updates, security notices).
  • Troubleshoot and enhance reliability (diagnostics only if enabled).
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

5) AI providers and other processors

  • AI answering: We transmit only your question and selected snippets to [AI provider name(s)] to generate an answer. We require providers to use this data solely to provide the requested output, and we do not allow providers to use your content to train their models without your explicit opt‑in. See provider policies for details.
  • Cloud backup (optional): If enabled, encrypted data is stored with [cloud provider].
  • Diagnostics (optional): If enabled, crash/performance data may be sent to [analytics/diagnostics provider].

We do not sell your personal information.

6) Legal bases / why we're allowed to process

For U.S. users, we process information to perform the contract (providing the Service), to maintain security and prevent abuse, and with your consent for optional features (e.g., cloud backup, diagnostics, marketing).

7) Retention

  • On‑device data: Stays on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • Cloud backup: Retained while sync is enabled and for a limited period after you disable it to allow recovery; then deleted from our systems within [X] days.
  • Logs/diagnostics: Retained for [X] days/months for troubleshooting if enabled.
  • Support emails: Retained for [X] months unless you request deletion.

8) Your choices & controls

  • Export: You can export your notes from within the app.
  • Delete: You can delete items locally; if cloud backup is enabled, deletions propagate after your next sync.
  • Opt‑in/opt‑out: You can enable/disable cloud backup, diagnostics, and marketing at any time.
  • AI disclosure: The app shows a concise explanation of what is sent for answering; you may choose not to use the Q&A feature.

9) U.S. state privacy rights (including California)

Depending on your state (e.g., California, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, Virginia), you may have rights to:

  • access, correct, or delete certain personal information;
  • receive a portable copy of information;
  • opt out of sales/sharing/targeted advertising (we do not sell or share personal information for cross‑context behavioral ads).

How to exercise rights: Email 2ndbrain.app@gmail.com with the subject "Privacy Request" and tell us what you'd like to do. We may need to verify your identity. Authorized agents: include proof of authorization. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

California notices:

  • Categories collected: Identifiers; account info; device data; in‑app content you provide; diagnostics (if enabled); purchase records (limited).
  • Sources: Directly from you and your device; App Store transactions; optional diagnostics provider.
  • Business purposes: Provide/maintain the Service; security; debugging; short‑term transient use for generating answers; servicing accounts; customer service; research and development.
  • Do Not Sell/Share: We do not sell or share personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA.
  • Sensitive information: Not sought; if you include sensitive info in your notes, it stays on device unless you enable backup or include it in a question that is sent as a snippet.

10) Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or older where required). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

11) Security

We use technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information. However, no system is perfectly secure. Keep your device and passcodes safe and enable OS‑level protections (e.g., Face ID). If we learn of a breach, we will notify you and regulators as required by law.

12) Do Not Track / Global Privacy Control

The Service does not respond to browser Do Not Track signals. If we ever engage in cross‑context behavioral advertising on the web, we will honor Global Privacy Control where required.

13) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If changes are material, we will notify you (e.g., in‑app, email). The "Effective" date at the top tells you when it last changed.

14) Contact

Questions or requests? Email 2ndbrain.app@gmail.com.
